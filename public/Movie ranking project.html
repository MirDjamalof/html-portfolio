Top 10 movies of all time

Pulp fiction

Experience the thrilling, darkly comedic world of "Pulp Fiction" where unforgettable characters and explosive twists redefine modern cinema!

Lord of the rings, return of the king

Witness the epic conclusion in "The Lord of the Rings: Return of the King," where heroes rise, battles rage, and Middle-earth's fate is decided!

Seven samurais

Join the timeless adventure of "Seven Samurai," where honor, bravery, and breathtaking action unite in Akira Kurosawa's legendary epic!

Good fellas

Step into the gripping world of "Goodfellas," where loyalty, power, and danger define the lives of mobsters in Martin Scorsese's crime masterpiece!

Once apon a time in west

Experience the wild frontier in "Once Upon a Time in the West," where Sergio Leone's epic tale of revenge, intrigue, and unforgettable characters unfolds against a breathtaking backdrop!

Spirited away

Immerse yourself in the magical world of "Spirited Away," where Hayao Miyazaki's enchanting tale of courage and friendship takes you on an unforgettable journey through a mystical realm!

KILL BILL vol.2

Dive into the thrilling finale of "Kill Bill: Volume 2," where Quentin Tarantino's fierce and relentless Bride continues her quest for vengeance in a gripping tale of action and redemption!

Full metal jacket

Enter the intense world of "Full Metal Jacket," where Stanley Kubrick's unflinching look at the brutality of war and the making of soldiers will leave you riveted and awed!

The apartment

Discover the charm of "The Apartment," where Billy Wilder's witty and heartwarming tale of love and ambition unfolds with timeless elegance and sharp social commentary.

Princess Monoke

Explore the mystical world of "Princess Mononoke," where Hayao Miyazaki weaves a powerful tale of nature, conflict, and transformation in a visually stunning and emotionally gripping adventure.
